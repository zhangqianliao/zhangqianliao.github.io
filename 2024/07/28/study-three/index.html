Three

That’s not how it happened at all. When I first brought Snoopy home, he was scared.

注:一个句子中如果出现了not在句尾出现了at all，这个句子中就包含“根本不是”这层意思。

You are not Sunny at all.

when:当…时候 first:第一，第一次 brought(过去式)bring：带来 was(过去式)is：是

scared：害怕的

Don’t be nervous.

nervous：紧张

You’re gonna like it here.

you’re gonna:你将要/将会… like:喜欢

I even got you a toy.

even：甚至 got(过去式)get：得到 toy：玩具

Maybe we’ll play with that later.

maybe：也许 play with：于…一起玩 later：一会

There you go. 给你/拿着

It’s your very own dog bad.

your very own：只属于你自己的

Snoopy？I thought you might like this better.

thought(过去式)think：：思考/认为 might：可能 better：表示更的意思

It‘s your very own doghouse.

Take a look inside.

inside:里面 take a look：看

bring：带来 bring…to sb.把…带给某人 bring…home 把…带回家 brought(过去式)

scared：感到害怕的 nervous：紧张 toy：玩具 later：一会儿

think：思考，认为 thought(过去式) I thought：我曾经认为…

your very own：只属于你自己的 take a look：看 inside：里面

take a look at sth.看看…东西(take a look at my new car.)
