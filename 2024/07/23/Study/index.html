Hexo
2024-07-23
Writing

A healthy baby girl,a princess was born.

baby：婴儿 girl：女孩 baby girl：女婴，女娃 baby boy：男婴，男娃

healthy：健康的 healthy food：健康食品

I’m very healthy. 我非常健康。 I’m not healthy.I’m ill. 我并不健康。我生病了。

princess：公主 prince：王子

was born：出生了 I was born in + 年份.

​ 我出生于某一年。

With beautiful golden hair.

beautiful：美丽的 golden：金色的 gold：黄金

To celebrate her birth,the King and Queen launched a flying lantern into the sky.

celebrate：庆祝 King：国王 Queen：王后

birth：出生 day：日子 birthday：生日

launch：发射 fly：飞 flying：会飞的，飞行的 lantern：灯笼

And for that one moment,everything was perfect.

moment：片刻 everything：一切事物 perfect：完美的 was：是

And then that moment ended.

end：结束

Gothel broke into the castle,stole the child,and just like that…gone.

broke(过去式) break：打破，打坏 into：进去…

broke into：闯入…地方 castle：城堡 break into my room.：闯入我的房间。

stole(过去式) steal：偷 child：孩子 gone：走了，离开了

The Kingdom searched and searched,but they could not find the Princess.

search：搜寻，寻找 search + 地方 = 搜寻一个地方

Why can’t I go outside? 为什么我不能去外面？

The outside world is a dangerous place.

dangerous：危险的 place：地方

Filled with horrible, selfish people.

fill：装满，填满 Filled with…：充满了….

horrible：糟糕的 bad：坏的，糟糕的（horrible表示：very very very bad）

selfish：自私的 people：人

You must stay here,where you’re safe.

stay：待着，停留 here：这里 stay here：在这待着，在这停留

safe：安全的

Do you understand,flower?

understand：明白，理解
