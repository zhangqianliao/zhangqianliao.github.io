study-Four

Hexo
2024-07-30
Writing

Excuse me.

You don’t have to be scared of me. I won’t hurt you.

have to：必须 be scared of：害怕… won’t：不会 hurt：害怕

Scared?Who said anything about being scared?

who：谁 said(过去式)say：说 about：关于 being：（状态是）

Hi.I’m Melody.

This here’s Tip, and I’m Dash.

this：这个 here：这里

You just blew our cover.

blew(过去式)blow：吹 cover：盖子，遮盖物 blew our cover：暴露身份

I was just trying to be friendly.

try：尝试 friendly：有好的 friend：朋友

Uh,guys,I really need to get going.So, if you could just tell me how…

guy：男人 guys在口语中表示”你们”，特别是打招呼 Hi,guys.

really：真的 need to：需要做某事 get going：离开

See?Now she’s gonna leave,and it’s all your fault.

leave：离开 …is gonna do sth.将要做某事 fault：某人的过错/过失

My fault?

Gays…

Well,maybe she doesn’t like birds that can’t fly.

bird：鸟

Gays…

Well,you can’t fly either.

either：也(用在否定句时)

And you walk funny.

walk：走路 funny：好笑的

Guys…

Oh,yeah?Well,you can’t fly either.

Guys…Do either of you know how to get to Atlantica？

either of you：两个中任意一个 know：知道

I don’t have much time.

much：很多 time：时间

Why do you want to go to Atlantica?

If i don’t, I’ll turn back into..

turn：转 turn..into..：把…变成… turn back into:变回 back：回去

A what?Turn back into a what?

A human.

human：人，人类 mermaid：美人鱼
